Before you order.

Questions, answered.

Are these real polarized — or just tinted?

Real polarized — not a dark tint. The proof: hold the lens to a phone screen and rotate it, and the screen blacks out. Tinted lenses can't do that. Every pair is UV400, too.

What if it's fake or not really polarized?

Then it's free. Every Dubery pair is genuinely polarized — do the phone-screen test yourself. If any pair we send you isn't true polarized, you get your money back. No argument.

Why is one pair ₱598 when it says ₱499?

₱499 is the pair. Add ₱99 shipping and it's ₱598 at your door — no COD fee. Order 2 pairs (₱998) and shipping is free — that's just ₱499 each.

Do you deliver nationwide? Is it COD?

Yes — cash on delivery nationwide, no COD fee. Next-day in Metro Manila via Gogo Express, a few days for the provinces. No prepayment; you pay when it arrives. Need it rush? Just tell us it's urgent and we'll do our best.

Which frame fits me?

Three shapes: Bandits (slim, vintage square), Outback (everyday), and Rasta (oversized, laid-back). Message us your usual fit and we'll point you to the right one.

How do I order?

Message us on Facebook or Instagram, or use the order form. Confirm your color and address, then pay COD when it lands. That's it.
