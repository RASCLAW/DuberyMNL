☀️ GLARE PROTECTION

Regular shades just darken your view. Polarized lenses actually block glare -- the reflected light that causes squinting and eye strain.

Blocks 100% of UVA and UVB rays. Your eyes take damage even on cloudy days. Polarized handles both.
