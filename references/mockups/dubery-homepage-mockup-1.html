How does Cash on Delivery work?
Simple: you order, we ship, you pay the rider when the package is in your hands. No bank account, no e-wallet, no upfront payment needed. Single pairs carry a ₱50 COD fee — order 2+ pairs and we waive it completely.
How fast is delivery?
Metro Manila: same-day for orders confirmed before early afternoon — lunchtime order, dinner-time shades. Nationwide via courier: typically 2–5 business days depending on your province. We send tracking once it ships.
How do I know the lenses are really polarized?
Test it yourself the moment it arrives: look at your phone screen through the lens, then rotate the sunglasses 90°. If the screen goes dark — that's a polarized filter working. Tinted lenses can't do that. Every Dubery pair passes, every time. That's the whole point of the brand.
Will they fit my face?
All three series are a medium unisex fit that suits most Filipino face shapes. Rough guide: Bandits for slimmer faces, Outback for medium-to-wide, Rasta sits comfortably in between. Not sure? Send us a photo on Messenger and we'll recommend honestly.
What if there's a problem with my pair?
Message us within 7 days of delivery for any defect or wrong item and we'll replace it — no drama, no interrogation. Every pair also ships with a hard case, pouch, and cleaning cloth, so it survives your bag.
What's included in the box?
The full kit: your sunglasses, a hard Dubery box, soft drawstring pouch, microfiber cleaning cloth, and a polarized test card. Everything in the photos is everything you get.
